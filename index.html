Vietnam's Internet Resources

Innovation Through Resolution 57-NQ/TW

What Are National Internet Resources?

  • .vn domain: Vietnam's digital identity
  • IP/ASN: Building blocks of network connectivity
  • DNS: Name resolution system of the Internet
  • VNIX: National Internet Exchange improving performance

Why Do They Matter?

  • Enable innovation and digital creativity
  • Support cybersecurity and data sovereignty
  • Power IoT, 5G, AI and other emerging technologies
  • Strengthen the digital economy

5 Innovative Ideas

.vn Startup Program: Domain support + website training for young innovators
IPv6 Hackathons: Encourage students to build apps using IPv6 only
Blockchain DNS Sandbox: Test decentralized name systems securely
AI-Powered VNIX Monitoring: Detect anomalies, optimize network flow
Digital Twin Infrastructure: Simulate major network changes before deployment
