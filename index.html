Innovation Through Vietnam's Internet Infrastructure

Empowering Creativity and Breakthroughs via Resolution No. 57-NQ/TW

Role of National Internet Resources

National Internet resources such as the .vn domain, IP/ASN, DNS, and the VNIX play a foundational role in realizing Vietnam’s goals of innovation and digital transformation. These elements are essential for a sovereign, stable, and future-ready digital infrastructure.

  • .vn domain: As Vietnam’s official top-level domain, it builds digital trust and identity for local businesses and organizations.
  • IP/ASN: Ensure reliable routing and global connectivity for Vietnam’s networks and digital services.
  • DNS: Vital for secure and efficient domain name resolution, a core function of internet usability.
  • VNIX: Facilitates faster, more secure internet exchange between domestic networks, reducing reliance on foreign bandwidth.

Driving Innovation and Breakthroughs

Aligned with Resolution No. 57-NQ/TW, which promotes national digital infrastructure as a lever for innovation, these resources enable:

  • Accelerated development of AI, IoT, and 5G applications
  • Improved national cybersecurity and data sovereignty
  • Lowered entry barriers for startups and local innovators
  • Digital economic growth through trusted online identities

Innovative Ideas for Effective Use

.vn Startup Launchpad: Offer free .vn domains and web hosting to student-led startups.
IPv6 Innovation Contests: Sponsor annual hackathons to encourage IPv6-native solutions.
DNS Blockchain Sandbox: Enable safe testing of blockchain-based DNS alternatives.
VNIX AI Optimization: Deploy AI to optimize traffic routing and detect anomalies in real time.
Digital Twin Infrastructure: Create virtual simulations of national internet infrastructure to model and test innovations before live deployment.
