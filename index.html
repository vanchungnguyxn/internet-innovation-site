Role of National Internet Resources

  • .vn domain: Vietnam's digital identity
  • IP/ASN: Reliable routing for global network
  • DNS: Name resolution system of the Internet
  • VNIX: Local internet exchange for better speed

Driving Innovation

  • Boosts digital creativity and economic growth
  • Supports national cybersecurity and sovereignty
  • Enables AI, 5G, IoT development
